GROSS DOMESTIC PRODUCT ANALYSIS :
teammates and roles :

Subba reddy venkat

Shiva kumar

Srivatsava


motive :

In Power bi tool this analysis examines the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of different countries and identifies trends and patterns in their economic growth over time. The project utilizes data from various sources to provide a comprehensive overview of the current state of the global economy, including factors such as GDP per capita, inflation rates, and trade balances. The analysis aims to provide insights into the factors that drive economic growth and to identify the key challenges facing countries in their efforts to achieve sustained economic development. The findings of the analysis can be used to inform policy decisions and to help countries develop strategies for improving their economic performance.
